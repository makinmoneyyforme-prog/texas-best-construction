One of the first questions every barndo owner faces: do you want a **shell** you finish on your own timeline, or a **turnkey** home we hand you the keys to? Both are great paths — the right one depends on your budget, your timeline, and how hands-on you want to be.

## The shell route

A shell is the sturdy steel-and-wood-framed base — weather-tight and ready for you to finish inside. It's the most flexible option if you're comfortable managing interior trades or want to spread the work out over time.

## The turnkey route

Turnkey means we handle everything from design to final finishes. You make the big decisions, we manage the rest, and you move into a finished home.

## How to choose

If you value control and have the time, a shell can stretch your budget further. If you'd rather hand off the complexity, turnkey keeps it simple. We're happy to talk through both for your land and goals.
