Ask ten people what a barndominium costs and you'll get ten answers. That's because the headline "price per square foot" hides most of what actually drives the budget. Here's where the real money goes.

## Site and dirt work

Before anything goes vertical, the land has to be ready. Clearing, pad prep, and utilities can swing a budget more than most people expect — especially on raw or sloped lots.

## Finish level

The same footprint can vary wildly depending on finishes. Standard, premium, and luxury selections — flooring, cabinetry, fixtures — are where a turnkey number really takes shape.

## Shell vs turnkey

A weather-tight shell costs far less than a finished home, because you're taking on the interior. It's the single biggest lever on the total.
